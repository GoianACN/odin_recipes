Lasagna

An Italian baked dish comprising layers of such pasta with various ingredients

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
